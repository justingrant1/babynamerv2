## Best Practices for Link Placement

### DO:
✅ Use natural, descriptive anchor text  
✅ Place links within valuable, relevant content  
✅ Make links contextually appropriate  
✅ Vary anchor text (not always exact keyword)  
✅ Link to different pages (not just homepage)  

### DON'T:
❌ Use generic anchors like "click here"  
❌ Stuff keywords unnaturally  
❌ Place all links in one paragraph  
❌ Make links look spammy or forced  
❌ Link to irrelevant pages
